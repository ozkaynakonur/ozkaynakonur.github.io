About

I am a PhD candidate in applied linguistics and TESOL at The Ohio State University. My research examines accentism, native-speakerism, Global Englishes, and language ideologies in TESOL teacher education.

Research Interests

  • Accentism and native-speakerism
  • Global Englishes and raciolinguistics
  • TESOL teacher education
  • Critical discourse analysis
