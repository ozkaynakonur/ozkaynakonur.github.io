Onur Özkaynak

PhD Candidate in Multilingual Language Education
The Ohio State University

I am a PhD candidate in Multilingual Language Education and a Graduate Teaching Associate in the Intercultural English Language Programs (IELP).

My research examines how language ideologies—particularly accentism and native-speakerism— shape judgments of legitimacy and competence in English language education.

Teaching

My teaching focuses on language teacher education and multilingual writing across undergraduate, graduate, and adult education contexts.

Research & Methodological Expertise

My research adopts mixed-methods designs grounded in critical sociolinguistics, integrating quantitative and qualitative approaches.
